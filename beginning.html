Beginning

Having recently escaped the Land of the Lotus Eaters, Odysseus and his crew were still travelling through mysterious lands on their way back home.

During one stormy night, the crew arrived at the mysterious Land of the Cyclopes, a race of one-eyed giants. As they had done throughout their journey, the crew robbed and pillaged the livestock of the Cyclopes.

Eventually, they came across an island with a large cave, inside which they found a flock of large sheep and crates of milk and cheese. An absolute feast! However, they were discovered by the cave's resident, the giant Cyclops Polyphemus! Although initially friendly, Polyphemus showed his monstrous side when he devoured several of Odysseus's men on the spot, and left the rest to eat later! What were they to do?
